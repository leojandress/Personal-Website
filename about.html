About Me

Over the years I started to fall in love with jazz music, specifically bebop. My favorite artists are Charlie Parker, Cannonball Adderley, and John Coltrane!

I played the medium sized saxophone called the Alto Saxophone. I was able to play Tenor for a little while, but ended up back on the Alto. There just wasn’t anything like the Alto, the range and control I had on the instrument was unmatched to the others.

My favorite performance art is probably concert band because the music felt elegant and beautiful. A close second would be jazz for sure. Improvising created so much room for spontaneous creativity.
