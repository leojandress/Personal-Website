Welcome!

I'm Leo Andress, and I played the saxophone for 7 years.

Marching band for 5 years, pep band for 5 years, concert band for 7 years, and jazz band for 4 years.

I really enjoyed playing saxophone. So much that I wanted to make playing it a career, but unfortunately I didn’t realize how competitive it was.

My skills were not up to par with other students my age at the time and I got so nervous before auditions. It was very heartbreaking to come to terms with my reality. I decided to instead play the saxophone as a hobby instead. I felt like this path would be more fulfilling rather than slaving away at the score for 9 hours a day, 7 days a week.

My love for the saxophone is still very strong and I play it from time to time, but only for fun. This site celebrates my joy and love for the saxophone!
